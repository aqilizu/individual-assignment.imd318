Education

My Educational Journey

Throughout my life, education has been a pivotal part of my growth. From my early years in school to my time at university, each step has shaped who I am today.

Primary School

Sekolah Rendah Agama Ar-Ridhwaniah, located in Bagan Serai, Perak, is where I completed my primary education. This school combines religious studies with academic subjects, making it a unique place to learn and grow. We studied Quranic studies, Arabic, Fiqh, and other Islamic teachings, alongside Mathematics, Science, and Bahasa Malaysia. The school instilled strong discipline, moral values, and a sense of community, shaping us for both religious and academic success in the future.

High School

SABK Maahad Al-Tarbiah Al-Islamiah is likely a Sekolah Agama Bantuan Kerajaan (SABK) in Malaysia, it appears to be an Islamic educational institution in Malaysia under the SABK system. These schools typically emphasize Islamic teachings alongside academic subjects, preparing students for both religious and mainstream education pathways.

University

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is one of Malaysia's largest public universities, offering a wide range of programs at diploma, degree, and postgraduate levels. It focuses on providing education and skills development, particularly for Bumiputera students, and has multiple campuses across the country. UiTM is known for its emphasis on practical skills and professional expertise.
